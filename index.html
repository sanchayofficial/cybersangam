Our Ideology

CyberSangam is different from other meetups because

Opportunities for All

Whether you’re a student or a senior, you get space, voice, and respect.

Learning over Branding

No promotions of companies or tools during talks — focus stays purely on knowledge.

Local & Regular

Regularly held in Delhi NCR to build a strong, consistent local cybersecurity community.

No Politics, No Fuss

We stay clear of internal scandals or favoritism often seen in other events.

frequently asked questions

Find Answers to Your Questions Instantly

What is CyberSangam?

CyberSangam is a brand new open source and volunteer driven Meetup Community in Delhi-NCR with a clear goal in mind.

Is CyberSangam Free?

Yes CyberSangam is absolutely free for everyone. We never charge anything from our participants, niether do we pay to our speakers. It's a volunteer driven platform, open for everyone.

What knowledge and pre-requisites are needed to attend CyberSangam Meetup?

You can come with an open mindset to learn. The meetup is suitable for all kind of people - newbie, professionals, college students or people from different fields.
